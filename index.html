About Us

Who are We?

My name is Donald (Duck) Tisdale and I am the proud owner of Tisdale's Southern BBQ. I grew up in a small town near Georgetown, South Carolina. As I watched my mother and father compete against each other in the kitchen, I developed a passion for cooking. This food on wheels is a dream come true for me and truly a gift from God. My family and I serve BBQ southern style, and some of everything, with our meats smoked for 10 hours and homemade food at affordable prices. I am thrilled as I watch our guests smile from enjoyment of eating what we have prepared. We are a family run business and we aim to please all of our guests and value their opinions. Be sure to check our social media pages and catering to see what we enjoy.
